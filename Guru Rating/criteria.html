Criteria

  • One star:
    If a guru has been involved in any sexual affairs with students, has abused or manipulated them, he immediately gets 1 star.
  • Two stars:
    There are possible signs that a guru has done any of the above but no conclusive evidence. There are always people who dislike a guru and may try to disgrace him even if he is actually genuine. But if there is a lot of drama in a community such as ex members who say the guru mistreated them, strange stories or members who were excommunicated, this is a red flag.
  • Three stars:
    If ...
  • Four stars:
    Good guru, but some possible signs of being commercial.
  • Five stars:
    No sign of: 1) Commercial interests
    2) Sexual affairs (with students)
    If any such actions become known, it may go from 5 stars to 1 star.
